Tourist Spots in Pakistan

The Hunza Valley is a mountainous valley located in the northern region of the Gilgit-Baltistan,Pakistan.

Gilgit is a city in Pakistani-administered Gilgit-Baltistan in the disputed Kashmir region.


Kashmir is the northernmost geographical region of the Indian subcontinent.

Kumrat is an alpine valley of the Dir Kohistan region in the Upper Dir District, Khyber Pakhtunkhwa province of Pakistan.


Skardu is a city located in Pakistan-administered Gilgit-Baltistan in the disputed Kashmir region.

Chitral is a city situated on the Chitral River in northern area of Khyber Pakhtunkhwa.


Neelum is a district of Pakistan-administered Azad Kashmir in the disputed Kashmir region.

Broghil Valley National Park is located in the upper northern reaches of the Upper Chitral District, Khyber Pakhtunkhwa, Pakistan, close to the Afghan-Pakistan border.


Gabin Jabba, which in Pashto means Honey marshes, is located approximately 65 km from Mingora on Matta Sakhra road within the Swat valley of the Khyber Pakhtunkhwa province of Pakistan.

The Allai Valley is bounded by Kohistan on the north and east by the Kaghan valley, Nandhiarh and Deshi of Deshiwals on the south, and by the Indus river on the west.
